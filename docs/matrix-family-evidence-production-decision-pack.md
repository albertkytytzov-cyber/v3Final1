# Matrix Family Evidence Production Decision Pack

Stage: Matrix Family-Level Evidence Review + Coach UI + Controlled Pilot Readiness.

## Current Decision

Matrix can remain a controlled pilot plan builder for safe, coach-editable
training content. It is not ready to become production default.

## Reviewed Families

AI desk review now covers all 26 evidence families through family source review
entries. P0 and P1 family dossiers document allowed use, blocked use,
limitations and next review actions.

## Allowed Now

- coach-editable controlled-pilot training content for safe training families;
- review-export summaries;
- warning/review-required prompts;
- quality logging for controlled pilot scenarios.

## Still Blocked

- rapid weight cut;
- dehydration or weigh-in manipulation;
- RED-S diagnosis;
- injury-return or pain clearance;
- youth high-load or weight-cut automation;
- BFR/KAATSU prescription;
- numeric medical, weight-cut or hydration gates.

## Production Boundary

Matrix remains feature-flagged and controlled-pilot only. Legacy fallback
remains default. Production route behavior, DB schema, rollout gates, preview
behavior and save/assign production paths remain unchanged.

Next stage is either real human review or limited pilot monitoring with coach
feedback.
