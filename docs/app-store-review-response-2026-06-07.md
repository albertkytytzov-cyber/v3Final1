## Guideline 3.2 — Business Distribution

PERFORM position:

- PERFORM is not restricted to a single company, club, federation, or employer.
- The app is intended for coaches, athletes, teams, and sports organizations as a general sports training platform.
- Any coach, athlete, team, club, or organization can become a client and use the app.
- Users can obtain an account through in-app registration or through coach/team onboarding.
- The public features include athlete readiness, training plan viewing, training execution tracking, device/Apple Health context, and coach-athlete workflow.
- There is no paid digital content inside the app at this stage.
